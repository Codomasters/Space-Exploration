Northrop
Grumman

  • Northrop Grumman Corporation is an American global aerospace and defense technology company.

  • With 90,000 employees and an annual revenue in excess of $30 billion, it is one of the world's largest weapons manufacturers and military technology providers.

  • The firm ranks No. 96 on the 2020 Fortune 500 list of America's largest corporations.

  • Northrop Grumman is made up of four main business sectors: Aeronautics Systems, Defense Systems, Mission Systems and Space Systems.
