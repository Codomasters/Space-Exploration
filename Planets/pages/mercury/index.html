Mercury

The First, and The Smallest

  • Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the sun in the Solar System.

  • Its orbit around the Sun takes 87.97 Earth days, the shortest of all the planets in the Solar System.

  • It is named after the Greek god Hermes (Ερμής), translated into Latin as Mercurius (Mercury), god of commerce, messenger of the gods, mediator between gods and mortals.
