Mars

The Red Planet

  • Mars, the fourth planet from the sun, is a dusty, cold, desert world with a thin atmosphere.

  • This dynamic planet has seasons, polar ice caps, extinct volcanoes, canyons, and weather.

  • One of the most explored bodies in the solar system, Mars is the only planet where we've sent rovers to roam the alien landscape.
