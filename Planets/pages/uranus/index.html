Uranus

The Sideways Planet

  • Uranus is the seventh planet from the sun.

  • It has the third-largest radius of all the planets.

  • It has 13 faint rings and 27 small moons.

  • But a characteristic that sets Uranus apart; It spins on its side as it orbits the sun.

  • That journey takes about 84 Earth years.
